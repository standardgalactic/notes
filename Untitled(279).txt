There is attestation of this kind of reduplicative article, often caused by language transfer and change over time. For examine The Euphrates, comes from Greek mishearing if al-fradat (The fradat), as Eu-phrates (good phrates) so the Euphrates would be literally "The The phrates) or The Assyrians which is "The as-syrians" where as is a gemminated "al" so it would be "The the-syrians" in literally translated English.
