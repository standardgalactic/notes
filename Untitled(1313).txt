Root

ه د ي • (h-d-y)

related to guidance, showing way

Derived termsEdit

Verbs

Active participle: هَادٍ‎ (hādin)

Passive participle: مَهْدِيّ‎ (mahdiyy)

Active participle: مُهَادٍ‎ (muhādin)

Passive participle: مُهَادًى‎ (muhādan)

Form IV: أَهْدَى‎ (ʔahdā, “to submit”)

Verbal noun: إِهْدَاء‎ (ʔihdāʔ)

Active participle: مُهْدٍ‎ (muhdin)

Passive participle: مُهْدًى‎ (muhdan)

Form V: تَهَدَّى‎ (tahaddā, “to be guided”)

Passive participle: مُتَهَدًّى‎ (mutahaddan)

Passive participle: مُتَهَادًى‎ (mutahādan)

Form VIII: اِهْتَدَى‎ (ihtadā, “to be directed”)

Verbal noun: اِهْتِدَاء‎ (ihtidāʔ)

Passive participle: مُهْتَدًى‎ (muhtadan)

Form X: اِسْتَهْدَى‎ (istahdā, “to seek the right path”)

Verbal noun: اِسْتِهْدَاء‎ (istihdāʔ)
