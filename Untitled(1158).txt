Certainly! Here are movie suggestions that align with each of the proposed plot themes:

1. **Cybersecurity Team vs. Hackers**: "Blackhat" - A furloughed convict and his American and Chinese partners hunt a high-level cybercrime network.

2. **Detective vs. Master Criminal**: "Catch Me If You Can" - An FBI agent pursues a young con artist who successfully impersonated an airline pilot, doctor, and legal prosecutor.

3. **Magician vs. Audience**: "The Prestige" - Two stage magicians engage in competitive one-upmanship in an attempt to create the ultimate stage illusion.

4. **Professional Negotiator vs. Stubborn Opponent**: "Inside Man" - A police detective, a bank robber, and a high-power broker enter high-stakes negotiations after a criminal's brilliant heist spirals into a hostage situation.

5. **Innovative Startup vs. Industry Giants**: "The Social Network" - The story of how Mark Zuckerberg created Facebook while attending Harvard and how it quickly became a global phenomenon.

6. **Conservationists vs. Poachers**: "The Last Lions" - A documentary following a lioness in Botswana's Okavango Delta as she battles to protect her cubs against the daunting onslaught of enemies.
